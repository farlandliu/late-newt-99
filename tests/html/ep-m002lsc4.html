Episode details

Radio 2,11 Nov 2025,147 mins

Christmas check-in

OJ Borg

Available for 22 days

OJ is live through the night from the Salford Riviera as he asks whether you are 'ho ho ho' or 'ho ho no'. Plus, Midnight Mastermind, Wish You Were There and the first Midnight Caller dial of the week. Midnight Mastermind is the quiz where you ask the questions and test OJ on your own specialist subject. He never knows the subject beforehand and has just one song to revise before facing the questions. Do you want to take part and quiz OJ? Wish You Were There is your chance to soundtrack a story or situation that OJ will tell you all about. He wants your suggestions for the perfect song to capture that moment. Midnight Caller is where OJs sent a basic mobile phone out to New Zealand, and calls it at 2.15am exactly. But who will answer it next? Will it get answered? Listen to find out! To get involved with the show, e-mail OJ@bbc.co.uk To listen on most smart speakers, just say "Ask BBC Sounds to play OJ Borg." Produced in Salford by Audio Always for BBC Radio 2
